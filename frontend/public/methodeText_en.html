My Method

My work is based on three core pillars:

1. A safe, supportive, and stable environment – I pay special attention to ensuring that the work takes place within secure boundaries.

2. We move at my clients’ own pace, because I know that we are all different. That’s why I don’t rush, push, or pressure anyone.

3. Thinking together – there are no one-size-fits-all solutions here. Throughout the process, I walk alongside those who turn to me. Since each person experiences their situation differently, the outcome and the path to resolution are also unique.

These three factors provide a framework in which we can bring out the best from my clients’ situations together.
